Spotify Wrapped

  • Go To Site

This app was also built during my second semester at Tech with five of my peers. Our goal was to create an application with functions similar to that of Spotify Wrapped, except with much more in-depth analysis of the user's listenning profile. Our "wraps" can be generated year-round, instead of only in December, and the time frame can be selected as well by logging in with their Spotify credentials. Additionally, you can add other users as friends and create joint wraps, view their old wraps, and make your own history public or private to others.
